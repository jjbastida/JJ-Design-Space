Endless Moji

Is a Japanese learning tool game designed to help users learn to read and differentiate between the first few japanese characters. It will be focusing primarily on the first group of Hiragana (the primary phonetic alphabet used in Japanese).

The game is played by simply pressing the letter that corresponds to the letter on screen before the timer runs out. If the timer runs out you lose one of your three lives. If you lose your three lives, it's game over.
